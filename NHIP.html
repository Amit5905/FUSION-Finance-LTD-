Welcome to Fusion Finance LTD Induction Programme

NHIP Day 01 - Overview and Introduction

Day 01 focuses on understanding Fusion Finance LTD, its mission, and the basics of microfinance. The sessions cover:

  • **Introduction to Fusion Finance LTD**: Vision, mission, and goals.
  • **Overview of Microfinance Industry**: The importance of financial inclusion and how microfinance empowers low-income households.
  • **Core Values**: Transparency, customer-centricity, and operational excellence.
  • **Understanding Customer Profiles**: Identifying the needs and challenges faced by our customers.
  • **Joint Liability Group (JLG) Model**: Basics of JLG formation, benefits, and responsibilities of group members.

By the end of Day 01, trainees will understand Fusion Finance's impact and their role in the organization's mission.

NHIP Day 02 - Loan Processes and CGT

Day 02 dives into the loan processes, operational guidelines, and how to conduct **Compulsory Group Training (CGT)**. Key topics include:

  • **Loan Products**: Features, interest rates, repayment schedules, and customer eligibility.
  • **Operational Guidelines**:
    • Branch operational radius and approval protocols.
    • Credit appraisal process based on MFI norms.
  • **CGT Process**:
    1. Introduction and pledge: Explaining the importance of compliance with company rules.
    2. Training: Roles and responsibilities of JLG members, group discipline, and loan terms.
    3. Verification: House visits to validate customer details and financial needs.
    4. Feedback and preparation for the Group Recognition Test (GRT).

During CGT, staff ensure customers understand the loan process and their obligations, ensuring transparency and trust.

NHIP Day 03 - Compliance and Risk Management

Day 03 focuses on compliance, risk management, and operational excellence. The sessions include:

  • **Branch Audit and Compliance**:
    • Key audit parameters for branches and field staff.
    • Ensuring adherence to RBI and company policies.
  • **Fraud Prevention and Risk Mitigation**:
    • Identifying and restricting unauthorized agent involvement.
    • Guidelines for mitigating risks during center formation and loan disbursement.
  • **Effective Center Meetings**:
    • Importance of regular attendance and discussions.
    • Resolving customer queries during meetings.
  • **Performance Monitoring**:
    • Key performance metrics for field staff and branch managers.
    • Strategies to improve operational efficiency.

By the end of Day 03, trainees will be equipped with the knowledge and tools to maintain compliance, mitigate risks, and drive operational success.
